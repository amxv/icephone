## How It Works

### Section Header

**Three steps to a fully staffed phone operation.**

**1. Build your agent.**
Pick a role template -- cold calling, support, collections, appointment setting, onboarding, or retention -- then customize the voice, personality, and conversation style. Test it live from your browser before a single call goes out.

**2. Connect your phones and CRM.**
Bring your own numbers from Twilio, Telnyx, or Vonage. Sync leads from HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, or Pipedrive. Link your Cal.com calendar so agents can book meetings in real time.

**3. Launch and monitor.**
Start a campaign, set your calling schedule and retry rules, and let IcePhone work through the list. Every call is transcribed, summarized, and scored for sentiment -- all visible from a single dashboard.

> **Annotation:** The three-step format reduces perceived complexity. Each step pairs an action with a clear outcome. Step 1 emphasizes how easy it is to try before committing. Step 3 shows you stay in control.

## FAQ

### Section Header

**Common questions.**

---

**Do I need my own phone numbers?**
Yes. IcePhone connects to your existing Twilio, Telnyx, or Vonage account. You bring the phone numbers; IcePhone provides the AI agents that use them.

**Can agents handle both inbound and outbound calls?**
Yes. Configure agents for outbound campaigns, inbound support, or both. Each agent can be assigned to specific phone numbers and use cases.

**How does the knowledge base work?**
Upload documents -- PDFs, web pages, Google Docs, Word files, text files, or images -- and IcePhone processes them into a searchable library. During a call, your agent searches that library to find relevant answers and cites specific sources in its responses.

**Can agents book appointments during a call?**
Yes, with Cal.com integration. The agent checks real-time calendar availability, offers time slots, and confirms the booking -- all within the conversation.

**What happens if the agent can't handle a call?**
You can configure call transfer rules with specific phone numbers and instructions. The agent will hand off to a human when it hits its limits.

**How many languages are supported?**
Voice agents are available in 10 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Hindi, Arabic, and Japanese.

**Is my data isolated from other teams?**
Yes. IcePhone uses a multi-tenant architecture with team-based data isolation. Your leads, agents, campaigns, knowledge bases, and call records are completely separated from other organizations.

**Can I test an agent before deploying it?**
Yes. The platform includes browser-based test calls where you can talk to your agent in real time, see the live transcription, and evaluate its performance -- no phone line needed.

## Annotations Summary

**Voice and Tone:** Professional but direct. No fluff, no hype. The audience is ops leaders, sales managers, and founders who have been burned by overpromising AI tools. The copy respects their intelligence and focuses on what the product actually does.

**Structure rationale:** The page follows a persuasive narrative -- hook (hero), credibility (integrations bar), empathy (problem), simplicity (how it works), value (benefits), specificity (use cases and features), trust (FAQ), and close (final CTA). Each section builds on the one before it.

**CTA strategy:** "Sign In" is the primary CTA because this is a closed platform. "See IcePhone in Action" is the secondary CTA for visitors who want to evaluate before requesting access. Both appear in the hero and the final CTA section.

**What was intentionally left out:** No fabricated statistics, testimonials, or customer names. No pricing. No competitor comparisons by name. These can be added as real data becomes available.
